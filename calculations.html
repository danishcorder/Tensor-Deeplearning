Use of Tensors in Deep Learning

Tensors flow through layers of neural networks:

  • Input Layer: Accepts data as tensors
  • Hidden Layers: Perform tensor operations (convolution, dot product, activation)
  • Output Layer: Produces predictions as tensors

Frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow rely on tensors for storing data, applying operations, and training models.
